Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our land plots and buying process.

What documents should I verify before buying a plot?

Essential documents include:

  • Title Deed (7/12 extract)
  • Encumbrance Certificate
  • Approved Layout Plan
  • RTC & Mutation Documents
  • NOC from relevant authorities

What payment options do you offer?

We provide flexible payment solutions:

  • One-time payment (discount available)
  • Installment plans (3-24 months)
  • Bank loan assistance
  • NRI investment support

How do you ensure the plot's legal clearance?

Our rigorous verification process includes:

  • Title chain verification for 30+ years
  • Legal opinion from property lawyers
  • Verification of all approvals
  • No litigation guarantee
