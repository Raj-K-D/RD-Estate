Project Overview

An exclusive plotted development offering premium canal views in the growing Fursungi area

Vasant Park is our premium plotted development in the rapidly developing Fursungi area of Pune. Completed in 2025, this project offers 20 carefully curated residential plots with direct canal views and lush green surroundings.

The development features wide internal roads, underground utilities, and premium landscaping. With plot sizes ranging from 1500 to 2500 sq.ft., Vasant Park combines natural beauty with modern infrastructure in one of Pune's most promising locations.
