Project Overview

Strategically located plotted development with premium amenities and excellent connectivity

Vasant Park is one of our premium plotted developments in the rapidly growing Loni Kalbhor area, strategically located near MIT University. Completed in 2020, this project offers 40 meticulously planned residential plots with all modern amenities.

The development features 20ft wide internal roads, underground electrification, and a comprehensive water supply system. With plot sizes ranging from 1200 to 2400 sq.ft., Vasant Park caters to diverse home-building needs while maintaining premium quality standards.
