Project Overview

Balaji Vishwa is one of our flagship plotted developments in the rapidly growing Bhekrai Nagar area of Pune. Completed in 2018, this project set new standards for plotted developments with its premium infrastructure and thoughtful planning.

The development features wide 40ft internal roads, underground electrification, and a comprehensive water supply system. With over 400 plots ranging from 1200 to 2400 sq.ft., Balaji Vishwa caters to diverse home-building needs.

Key Features:

  • Premium 40ft wide internal roads with footpaths
  • Underground electricity with individual meters
  • 24/7 water supply with overhead tank
  • Comprehensive storm water drainage system
  • Green belt development with native plants
  • Children's play area and community space
